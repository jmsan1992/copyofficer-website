Three common approaches — and what each one misses.

01

The translation

Many teams start by translating their website into Spanish and launching a few campaigns. Traffic appears, but user behavior often stays unchanged.

Translation aligns language. Acquisition requires alignment in structure and decision flow.

02

The freelancer

Some companies bring in bilingual freelancers or small agencies to manage this segment. Individual pieces can perform well, but the overall system lacks continuity.

Without a unified structure, results don't accumulate over time.

03

The one-off campaign

Others test a focused campaign for a period. Engagement increases and traffic grows. Without infrastructure, the impact on revenue remains difficult to track and improve.

A system makes those signals usable.
